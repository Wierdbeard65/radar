Radar

A complete home management system (NOT automation!) Named after Radar in M*A*S*H who had everything filed away and knew what was needed and when

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Ever felt overwhelmed by paperwork? Sick of missing appointments? Fed up of trying to balance your checkbook?

Well, let Corporal "Radar" O'Reilly help!

There are plenty of document management, shared calendaring and accounting packages out there for business. But Radar is designed to help you get on top of your personal life. The various elements are interlinked so that it becomes a complete tool for the busy, modern family.

The system runs as a Windows service on a machine that is permanently on and connected to the internet (the Rader Server). You then connect to the server using clients to interact with it. Plans are to have clients for all the major platforms.

Radar can also be configured to gather information automatically via, for example, E-Mail. Making the import of information a snap.

So please, download, use comment become involved.
